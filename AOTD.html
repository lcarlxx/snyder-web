ARMY OF THE DEAD (2021)
Synopsis:

In Los Angeles, after an accident on the road between a truck of an army convoy that is transporting a top-secret cargo and a civilian car, the container with the cargo is released and a zombie is released. The zombie kills the soldiers and Los Angeles is attacked and most of the population is infected, soon becoming a city of zombies isolated from humans. When the government decides to wipe-out LA from the map using a nuke, the restaurant cook Scott Ward, who survived the attack, is invited by the casino owner Bly Tanaka to retrieve US 200 million from his state-of-art vault before the bombing; in return, he would receive US 50 million. Scott accepts the challenge and organizes a ten-member crew to accomplish the task. But Tanaka has a hidden agenda that will affect the mission.
