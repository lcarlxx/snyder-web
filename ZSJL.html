ZACK SNYDER'S JUSTICE LEAGUE (2021)
Synopsis:

Fueled by his restored faith in humanity and inspired by Superman's selfless act following the events of Batman V Superman, Bruce Wayne enlists newfound ally Diana Prince to face an even greater threat. Together, Batman and Wonder Woman work quickly to recruit a team to stand against this newly awakened enemy. Despite the formation of an unprecedented league of heroes -- Batman, Wonder Woman, Aquaman, Cyborg and the Flash -- it may be too late to save the planet from an assault of catastrophic proportions.
